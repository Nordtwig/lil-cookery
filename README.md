# Lil' Cookery

A cozy local co-op (2 players) restaurant sim. Prep ingredients, cook, plate dishes,
and serve customers at their tables - built in Godot 4.7. Still early/graybox; a
scoped-down learning project and stepping stone toward a bigger online restaurant sim.

## Playing

Two players, same keyboard or two controllers.

| Action | Player 1 | Player 2 |
|---|---|---|
| Move | WASD | Arrow keys |
| Interact (take/place/combine) | E | , |
| Action (skill-timing, e.g. the stove's flip catch) | R | M |
| Inspect | Q | . |

Or plug in two gamepads - controls are bound per-device automatically.

**The loop:** grab an order ticket from a waiting table, gather that dish's ingredients,
chop/cook as needed, plate it up, deliver it to the right table, then come back to
collect the cash once the customer's finished eating.

## Running it

Open the project in [Godot 4.7](https://godotengine.org/download) and hit Play - or
grab a build from the [Releases](../../releases) page if one's up.

## Status

Actively in development. Graybox art, no sound yet - the mechanics come first.
